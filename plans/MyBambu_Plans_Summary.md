## Overview

Four-tier assistance program for remittance services providing benefits to both **remittance senders** (in USA) and **remittance recipients** (in origin countries).

**Key Details**:
- Validity: 30 days per enrollment
- Minimum guarantee: 10,000 users/month (starting year 2)
- Revenue split: 70% to MERKA CORP, 30% to MY BAMBU
- Auto-renewal with 7-day grace period for payment
