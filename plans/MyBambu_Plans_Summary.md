## Overview

Four-tier assistance program for remittance services providing benefits to both **remittance senders** (in USA) and **remittance recipients** (in origin countries).

**Key Details**:
- Validity: 30 days per enrollment
- Minimum guarantee: 10,000 users/month (starting year 2)
- Revenue split: 70% to MERKA CORP, 30% to MY BAMBU
- Auto-renewal with 7-day grace period for payment

**Reimbursement Requirements**:
- Request within 72 hours
- Submit documentation:
  - Copy of valid passport/ID

  - Payment document
  - Bank details

- Body removal from place of death
- Legal formalities for burial/cremation permit

- Thanatopraxy (embalming and preparation)
- Wake service (up to 24 hours)
- Standard casket
- Floral arrangement
- Coffee service
- Obituary notice
- Hearse (up to 20km to church/cemetery)
- Religious service
- Burial assistance (vault for 4 years) OR cremation

**Eligibility**:
- Age: 18-75 at enrollment (no limit after)
- Waiting periods:
  - Violent/suspicious death: 24 hours
  - Natural death: 121 days
  - Catastrophic illness: 181 days

### Nutritional Teleassistance:
- Professional guidance on eating habits, supplements, body fat
- Business hours: 9 AM - 5 PM, Mon-Sat
- Requires 24-hour advance scheduling
- Up to 60 minutes

### Psychological Teleassistance:
- Topics: Anxiety, stress, parenting, relationships, grief, sleep disorders

- Up to 60 minutes
