# My Bambu Assistance Program - Marketing Strategy

**Continental Assist Integration | 12-Month Launch Strategy**

---

## ðŸ“‹ Executive Summary

**Objective**: Launch Continental Assist's 4-tier assistance program to My Bambu's customer base, achieving 26,284 paying subscribers and $945,421 in Year 1 revenue (30% share).

**Customer Base:**
- 115,000 monthly active customers
- 660,000 total accounts
- Target: 22.9% active penetration by Month 12

**Strategy:** Phased segment-specific approach prioritizing high-value active customers, then scaling to broader active base, and finally reactivating dormant accounts.

**Budget:** $350,000 Year 1
**Expected ROI:** 270% ($945K revenue on $350K spend)

## ðŸŽ¯ Target Segments & Personas

### Segment 1: High-Value Active "The Protector"

**Profile:**
- **Size**: 28,750 (25% of active base)
- **Behavior**: 3+ remittances/month, $300+ per transaction
- **Demographics**: Age 35-55, married, 2+ kids, has elderly parents
- **Psychographics**: Family-focused, responsible, worried about "what if"
- **Pain Points**:
  - Fear of unexpected death leaving family stranded
  - Worry about parents' health in home country
  - High cost of funeral repatriation ($10K+)

**Marketing Approach:**
- **Message**: "Protect your family when they need you most"
- **Focus**: $10K funeral coverage, telehealth for family
- **Channel**: Email, in-app, SMS, personal outreach
- **Offer**: 7-day free trial + $10 sign-up bonus
- **Target Plan**: Advanced+ ($15.99)

---

### Segment 2: Medium Active "The Balancer"

**Profile:**
- **Size**: 46,000 (40% of active base)
- **Behavior**: 1-2 remittances/month, $150-300 per transaction
- **Demographics**: Age 25-45, single or small family
- **Psychographics**: Budget-conscious but values peace of mind
- **Pain Points**:
  - Healthcare access limitations
  - Cost of medical care
  - Supporting family remotely

**Marketing Approach:**
- **Message**: "Healthcare for your whole family, just $7.99/month"
- **Focus**: Telehealth savings, family support
- **Channel**: Email, in-app, social proof
- **Offer**: 7-day free trial, first month 50% off
- **Target Plan**: Intermediate ($7.99) with upsell to Advanced

---

### Segment 3: Low Active "The Saver"

**Profile:**
- **Size**: 40,250 (35% of active base)
- **Behavior**: Occasional remittances, <$150 per transaction
- **Demographics**: Age 18-35, single, price-sensitive
- **Psychographics**: Needs convincing, skeptical of subscriptions
- **Pain Points**:
  - Tight budget
  - Doesn't think about "what if"
  - Never had travel assistance before

**Marketing Approach:**
- **Message**: "Just $5.99 for peace of mind - less than a coffee"
- **Focus**: Price, simplicity, Rx savings
- **Channel**: In-app, SMS, simple messaging
- **Offer**: 7-day free trial, no credit card required
- **Target Plan**: Basic ($5.99)

---

### Segment 4: Recently Inactive "The Lost Sheep"

**Profile:**
- **Size**: 109,000 (20% of total accounts)
- **Behavior**: Last transaction within 3 months
- **Demographics**: Various, but previously engaged
- **Psychographics**: Life got busy, switched services, or dissatisfied
- **Pain Points**:
  - Forgot about My Bambu
  - Found competitor
  - Had bad experience

**Marketing Approach:**
- **Message**: "We miss you! Come back with protection"
- **Focus**: Win-back incentive, new benefits
- **Channel**: Email, SMS, retargeting ads
- **Offer**: 30 days free + $20 remittance credit
- **Target Plan**: Match previous usage pattern

**Profile:**

## ðŸ“± Channel Strategy

### 1. Email Marketing (35% of budget)

**Why**: Highest ROI channel, owned audience, proven conversion

**Tactics:**

**High-Value Segment:**
- 5-email nurture sequence
- Day 0: Launch announcement from CEO
- Day 2: Social proof (testimonials)
- Day 5: Feature deep-dive ($10K funeral)
- Day 7: Last chance (urgency)
- Day 10: Final reminder

**Medium Segment:**
- 4-email sequence
- Day 0: Value proposition
- Day 3: Telehealth savings calculator
- Day 7: Customer stories
- Day 12: Limited time offer

**Low Segment:**
- 3-email sequence
- Day 0: Simple benefit explanation
- Day 7: "Most popular plan" recommendation
- Day 14: Price reminder + FAQ

**Inactive Segments:**
- Win-back sequence (6 emails over 8 weeks)
- Multi-incentive approach
- Personalized based on last activity

**Email KPIs:**
- Open rate: 35%+
- Click rate: 8%+
- Conversion rate: 5%+

---

### 2. In-App Promotion (25% of budget)

**Why**: Highest visibility, catches users at moment of intent

**Tactics:**

**Home Screen Banner:**
- Prominent placement
- Rotating messages by segment
- Click to learn more
- A/B test 5 variations

**Post-Transaction Interrupt:**
- After successful remittance
- "You just sent $300 - protect your family for $9.99"
- One-click enrollment
- Skip option available

**Account Section Widget:**
- Persistent visibility
- "Get Protected" call-to-action
- Show plan options

**Push Notifications:**
- 4 strategic push notifications
- Day 0: Launch announcement
- Day 3: "Your family deserves protection"
- Day 7: Last chance for sign-up bonus
- Day 14: "Join 5,000 protected families"

**In-App KPIs:**
- Banner CTR: 3%+

- Push open rate: 25%+
