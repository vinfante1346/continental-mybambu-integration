## üí∞ SELLING STRATEGIES & TACTICS

### Tactic 1: Consultative Selling Approach

**For Personal Calls to High-Value Customers**

**Call Structure (10-15 minutes):**

**1. Introduction (2 min)**
```
"Hi [Name], this is [Agent] from My Bambu. I'm calling because
you're one of our valued customers, and we've launched something
new that I think could really help your family. Do you have a few
minutes to chat?"
```

**2. Discovery Questions (3 min)**
```
- "Tell me about your family - do you have loved ones back in [Country]?"
- "How often do you send remittances to support them?"
- "Have you ever worried about what would happen if something
  unexpected occurred?"
- "Does your family have easy access to healthcare?"
```

**3. Tailored Solution (3 min)**
```
Based on responses, present relevant plan:

HIGH-VALUE SCRIPT:
"Based on what you've shared, I'd recommend our Advanced+ plan.
Here's why: You're supporting [X people] back home, and if something
happened to you, they'd be taken care of. Plus, your [mom/dad] can
see a doctor anytime from their phone - included."
```

**4. Objection Handling (3 min)**

**Objection: "It's too expensive"**
```
"I understand. Let me break it down: You send about $300/month
to support your family, right? This is $15/month - that's 5% of
what you already send. And it covers $10,000 in funeral costs,
which would otherwise cost you 30+ months of remittances. Plus,
one emergency doctor visit pays for the entire year."
```

**Objection: "I need to think about it"**
```
"Totally understand. Here's what I suggest: try it free for 7 days.
No credit card required. If you don't see the value, cancel anytime.
Plus, I'll add a $10 bonus to your account just for trying. Can I
set that up for you right now?"
```

**Objection: "I already have insurance"**
```
"That's great! This isn't insurance though - it's assistance. It
works alongside your insurance. For example, does your insurance
cover repatriation to [Country]? Does it give your family in [Country]
access to doctors? This fills those gaps."
```

**5. Close (2 min)**
```
"Here's what I'll do: I'll enroll you in the free trial right now.
You'll get an email with all the details. Try it for 7 days, book
a telehealth session, test it out. If you love it - which I think
```
