# Risk Analysis & Mitigation

## Key Risks

### Risk 1: Lower Than Expected Conversion
**Scenario**: Only 2% vs 3% actual
**Impact**: -33% users, -$185k ARR
**Mitigation**:
- Extend trial to 14 days
- Increase incentives ($10-20 credits)
- Improve onboarding & education
- A/B test messaging aggressively

---

### Risk 2: Higher Churn
**Scenario**: 8% vs 6% monthly churn
**Impact**: -25% user retention
**Mitigation**:
- Usage-based intervention (proactive outreach)
- Value reinforcement (savings reports)
- Win-back offers (pause vs cancel)
- Product improvements

---

### Risk 3: CAC Inflation
**Scenario**: $10 avg CAC vs $6.64
**Impact**: Reduced profitability margin
**Mitigation**:
- Still profitable at 3:1 LTV:CAC
- Focus on Always Active (lower CAC)
- Optimize campaign efficiency
- Leverage organic/referral channels

### Risk 4: Competitive Response
**Scenario**: Competitor launches similar offering
**Impact**: Slowed growth, pricing pressure
**Mitigation**:
- First-mover advantage (600k customer head start)
- Lock in users with annual contracts
- Bundle with Assistance Program
- Build switching costs (health history, provider relationships)
