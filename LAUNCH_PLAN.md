## ðŸš¨ Risk Management

### Key Risks & Mitigation

**Risk 1: Low Adoption Rate**
- **Mitigation:** Enhanced marketing, better value messaging, promotions
- **Contingency:** Price adjustments, feature enhancements

**Risk 2: High Churn Rate**
- **Mitigation:** Proactive engagement, retention campaigns, value reinforcement
- **Contingency:** Win-back offers, exit surveys, product improvements

**Risk 3: Loss Ratio Exceeds 60%**
- **Mitigation:** Careful underwriting, fraud detection, claim validation
- **Contingency:** Price increase, benefit adjustments, plan restructuring

**Risk 4: API Reliability Issues**
- **Mitigation:** Redundancy, caching, error handling, SLA monitoring
- **Contingency:** Backup manual processes, customer communication

**Risk 5: Regulatory Compliance**
- **Mitigation:** Legal review, compliance monitoring, documentation
- **Contingency:** Product modifications, state-specific exclusions

**Risk 6: Customer Confusion**
- **Mitigation:** Clear messaging, education, support training
- **Contingency:** Simplified tier structure, better documentation
